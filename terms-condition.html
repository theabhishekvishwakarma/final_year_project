Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: April 25, 2024

Welcome to Gramfo! By participating in our events, you agree to these terms that help us protect both our community and the planet.

1. Event Participation

  • All participants must follow environmental guidelines provided for each event
  • Proper safety gear must be worn at all times during cleanup activities
  • Minors under 16 require parental consent and supervision

2. Environmental Commitment

  • Participants must adhere to Leave No Trace principles
  • All waste collected must be properly sorted and disposed
  • Use of single-use plastics is prohibited during events

3. Registration & Payments

  • Registration fees (if applicable) are non-refundable
  • Free events require 24-hour cancellation notice
  • False information will result in participation ban

4. Media & Privacy

  • Gramfo may use event photos for promotional purposes
  • Personal data will never be shared with third parties
  • Participants can opt-out of photography
